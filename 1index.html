Bio

Avul Pakir Jainulabdeen Abdul Kalam, popularly known as APJ Abdul Kalam, was born on 15 October 1931 in Rameswaram, Tamil Nadu. He was the 11th President of India, serving from 2002 to 2007.

Kalam was a scientist and administrator, mainly known for his work on the development of ballistic missile and launch vehicle technology.

He was awarded the Bharat Ratna, India's highest civilian honor, in 1997.
